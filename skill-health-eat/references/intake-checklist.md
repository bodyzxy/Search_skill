# Intake Checklist / 信息确认清单

Use concise questions. Do not ask every item at once. Ask the smallest set needed for a safe recommendation.

## First Screen / 第一轮优先确认

Chinese:

1. 现在是想吃哪一餐，还是补一顿？上一餐什么时候吃的？
2. 年龄段与性别/生理状态：儿童、青少年、成年男/女、孕期/哺乳期、老年人？
3. 有没有已知疾病或正在用药？尤其糖尿病、低血糖风险、高血压、高血脂、高尿酸/痛风、肾病、肝病、胃肠病、心衰。
4. 有没有食物过敏、严格忌口、宗教/素食要求、明显挑食？
5. 今天/前天有没有熬夜、重体力劳动、长时间用脑、运动、饮酒、腹泻/呕吐？
6. 现在能做饭、点外卖、食堂、便利店，还是只有少量食材？预算大概多少？

English:

1. Which meal is this for, or are you making up for a skipped meal? When was your last meal?
2. What age/life stage and sex should I account for: child, teen, adult male/female, pregnant/lactating, older adult?
3. Any diagnosed conditions or medications, especially diabetes, hypoglycemia risk, hypertension, high cholesterol, gout/high uric acid, kidney/liver/GI disease, or heart failure?
4. Any allergies, strict avoidances, religious/vegetarian requirements, or strong dislikes?
5. Any recent sleep deprivation, heavy physical work, long cognitive work, intense exercise, alcohol, vomiting, or diarrhea?
6. What food environment and budget are realistic: home cooking, delivery, cafeteria, convenience store, limited ingredients?

## Ask When Relevant / 按需追问

- Diabetes: type, insulin or sulfonylurea use, current glucose if known, hypoglycemia history, carb target if prescribed.
- Hypertension/heart disease: sodium target, edema/fluid limit, potassium restrictions, heart failure status.
- Kidney disease: stage, dialysis, potassium/phosphorus/fluid/protein restrictions.
- Gout/high uric acid: current flare, alcohol intake, seafood/organ meat habit.
- Pregnancy/lactation: gestational diabetes, nausea, food safety concerns, caffeine intake.
- Children: exact age, caregiver approval, choking risks, growth concerns.
- Older adults: chewing/swallowing, appetite, constipation, weight loss, frailty, medication interactions.
- Allergies: allergen, severity, cross-contact sensitivity, epinephrine availability.
- Skipped meal: intentional fasting, medication timing, dizziness/shaking/sweating, next activity.

## If User Gives Very Little Context / 信息很少时

Give a safe short response plus 3 questions:

Chinese template:
"我可以先给一个保守选择，但为了更准确，需要确认：1. 你现在吃哪一餐/上一餐何时吃？2. 有没有疾病、用药或过敏？3. 你现在能做饭还是只能外卖/便利店？"

English template:
"I can suggest a conservative option first, but to tailor it safely I need: 1. Which meal is this and when did you last eat? 2. Any conditions, medications, or allergies? 3. Can you cook, order out, or only use convenience-store foods?"
